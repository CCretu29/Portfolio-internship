Project Process

In this section, I will explain the steps I took to complete the projects. This includes the initial planning, design, development, testing, and deployment phases. After creating a CV, I decided that it would be nice to also showcase my work in a portfolio. I made this portfolio in my free time in one of the holidays that I had. Due to time constrains and the trust that I have in my skills I designed and developed the portfolio in the same time. I started setting up the Angular project, witch took me a while because I was following commands given by the AI, and I had some errors. Afterward I decided to use the documentation from the official Angular website and that was easy and smooth. I learned the syntax quickly and I started developing the portfolio.
